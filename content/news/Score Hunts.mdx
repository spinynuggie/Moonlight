## What the hell is this?

- **Score Hunts** — Are a system where asteriaAI will set a score on a map and everyone on the server has a set time to snipe it.
- Whichever score ends up being #1 by the end of the score hunt will get a prize 

## What are the rewards?

Frankly we're still trying to figure that out (it's really hard to offer stuff when we already offer what supporter does for free)

We're thinking of stuff like temporary cosmetics, discord goodies and so on. We're open to suggestions!
