## What is this?

- **Score Hunts** are time-limited events where asteriaAI sets a score on a specific map with specific mods.
- Everyone on the server has a limited window to snipe it.
- By the end of the event, whoever holds the **#1 score** wins the hunt—and the reward.

Think of it as a live competition to snipe each other before time runs out.

## What are the rewards?

We’re still figuring this out.

It’s surprisingly tricky to design rewards when many of the usual perks (like supporter features) are already available for free. That said, we’re exploring ideas such as:

- Temporary cosmetics
- Discord perks and goodies
- Event-exclusive rewards

We’re absolutely open to suggestions—if you have ideas, let us know!
